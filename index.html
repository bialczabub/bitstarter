DynaDraft is a web-based fantasy football drafting tool that gives you the ultimate advantage during your league's draft. It dynamically examines your team and the remaining undrafted players and let's you know who is the best bet with your next pick. Don't spend your time flipping through magazines or scratching names off of paper cheatsheets. Spend it dominating.

DynaDraft works in any web browser regardless of device (PC, laptop, cell phone, tablet, etc.)
